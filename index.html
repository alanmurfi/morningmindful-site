Getting Started

Up and running in minutes

A simple setup flow gets you journaling by tomorrow morning.

1

Download & Install

Get Morning Mindful from the Google Play Store and open it to begin setup.

2

Configure Your Preferences

Choose your blocking duration, word count goal, morning hours, and blocking mode.

3

Grant Permissions

Enable Accessibility (Full Block) or Usage Access (Gentle) - only used to detect blocked apps.

4

Set Up Backup

Choose a folder and password for encrypted auto-backups. Restores instantly on reinstall.

5

Wake Up Mindfully

Tomorrow morning, blocked apps will prompt you to journal first. Write, reflect, unlock!

FAQ

Common Questions

Why does it need Accessibility permissions?

The Accessibility Service detects when you open a blocked app and redirects you. It does NOT read content from other apps - it only sees which app is in the foreground.

Is my journal data private?

Yes! All entries are encrypted with AES-256 and stored only on your device. We never see, collect, or transmit your journals. Your data belongs to you.

What if I uninstall the app?

If you have auto-backup enabled, your journals are safe in your chosen folder or Google Drive. After reinstalling, select the same backup source and we'll detect the backup and offer to restore it.

Why isn't blocking working?

Make sure you've granted the required permissions. On some phones, also disable battery optimization for Morning Mindful in Settings → Apps → Morning Mindful → Battery.

What languages are supported?

English, German, and Chinese (Simplified). The app uses your phone's language setting. Chinese characters are properly counted (1 character = 1 word).
