Privacy Policy for Morning Mindful

Last updated: March 21, 2026 | Platforms: Android & iOS

Introduction

Morning Mindful ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application on Android or iOS.

Information We Collect

Health and Wellness Data

Morning Mindful is a mental wellness and mindfulness app. The following data may be considered health-related data:

  • Mood selections: You may record your mood (e.g., happy, calm, anxious) with each journal entry. This data is stored on your device in an encrypted database and is never transmitted to any server we control.
  • Journal entries: Your written reflections may contain personal thoughts related to your mental health and wellbeing. This content is stored on your device in an encrypted database and is never read, accessed, or collected by us.
  • Journaling streaks and habits: The app tracks how many consecutive days you have journaled. This is stored locally for your personal motivation and is not shared.

Optional Backup of Health Data

Morning Mindful provides backup options to protect your journals. Regardless of the method you choose, your data is always encrypted before leaving your device:

  • Encrypted on your device before export using AES-256-GCM with a password only you know
  • Unreadable by us — the encrypted data cannot be decrypted without your backup password
  • Under your full control — you can delete your backups at any time

Android You can optionally back up to Google Drive, stored in a hidden, app-only folder (appdata scope). Google Drive backup is entirely optional and disabled by default.

iOS You can export encrypted backup files manually and store them wherever you choose.

How we handle your health data: All health and wellness data is encrypted with AES-256 whether stored on your device or backed up. We never collect, read, transmit, or share your health data. You have full control to export or delete your data at any time.

Other Information Stored on Your Device

Morning Mindful also stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Photo attachments: Images you attach to journal entries, stored locally on your device in app-private storage
  • App settings: Your preferences such as blocking duration, word count requirements, morning window times, and selected apps to block
  • Usage statistics: Entry counts, word counts, and streaks (stored locally for your personal tracking)
Important: All data is stored on your device using encrypted storage. We cannot read your journal entries or personal data.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect or transmit your journal entries to any server we control
  • We do not collect personal identification information
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not access your contacts or other personal files
  • We do not sell or share any data with third parties

How App Blocking Works

Morning Mindful blocks distracting apps during your configured morning window until you complete a journal entry. The blocking mechanism differs by platform:

Android Accessibility Service

On Android, Morning Mindful uses the Accessibility Service to detect when you open apps that you've chosen to block during your morning mindfulness period. This permission is used solely to redirect you to your journal when blocked apps are opened.

We do not:

  • Read or collect any content from other apps
  • Log which apps you use
  • Transmit any accessibility data off your device

Android Usage Stats Access (Gentle Reminder Mode)

If you choose Gentle Reminder mode on Android, Morning Mindful requests access to Usage Stats to detect which app is in the foreground and show a reminder overlay.

We do not:

  • Collect or store your app usage history
  • Track how long you use other apps
  • Transmit any usage data off your device

iOS Screen Time (FamilyControls & ManagedSettings)

On iOS, Morning Mindful uses Apple's Screen Time framework (FamilyControls, ManagedSettings, and DeviceActivity) to block apps you've selected during your morning window. A shield is displayed over blocked apps until you complete your journal entry.

We do not:

  • Access which specific apps you use or for how long
  • Collect or transmit any Screen Time data
  • Have access to your device usage beyond the blocking functionality

All Screen Time data stays on your device and is managed entirely by Apple's framework.

Other Permissions

Notifications Android iOS

Used for:

  • Showing a notification when app blocking is active
  • Sending optional daily reminder notifications at a time you choose

Camera & Photo Library iOS

Used only to attach photos to your journal entries. Photos are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded or shared.

Display Over Other Apps Android

Allows the app to show the journal screen or reminder overlay when you attempt to open a blocked app.

Boot Completed & Exact Alarms Android

Used to restart the blocking service and reschedule daily reminders after your device reboots.

Foreground Service Android

Used to keep the blocking monitor running reliably in the background during your configured morning window.

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob Android

The Android version of Morning Mindful displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain information for ad personalization. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device's Google settings.

Firebase Analytics Android

The Android version uses Firebase Analytics to understand how the app is used in aggregate (e.g., which features are popular, how often the app is opened). This service collects:

  • Anonymous usage events (e.g., "journal saved", "settings changed")
  • Device information (model, OS version, screen size)
  • App version and session duration

No personal data, journal content, or mood selections are included in analytics events.

Firebase Crashlytics Android

The Android version uses Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports and improve app stability. No personal data or journal content is included in crash reports.

Firebase Performance Monitoring Android

The Android version uses Firebase Performance Monitoring to track app performance. No personal data or journal content is included in performance reports.

iOS No Third-Party Analytics or Ads

The iOS version of Morning Mindful does not include any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. All data stays entirely on your device.

Data Security

All locally stored data is encrypted using industry-standard encryption:

  • Android SQLCipher for database encryption (AES-256) and Android Keystore-backed EncryptedSharedPreferences for settings and encryption keys
  • iOS SwiftData with iOS Data Protection (.completeFileProtection) and AES-256-GCM (CryptoKit) for backup encryption

Your journal entries and settings are protected with industry-standard encryption and cannot be accessed by other apps or by us.

Data Backup

Manual Export/Import Android iOS

  • Export all journals to an encrypted backup file anytime
  • Backups are encrypted with AES-256-GCM using a password you choose
  • We never see your backup password or data

Auto-Backup Android

  • Automatically saves your journals to a local folder you choose during setup
  • Encrypted with AES-256-GCM using a password you set
  • Backups remain on your device — we never have access

Optional Google Drive Backup Android

  • You may optionally choose Google Drive as your backup destination
  • Your data is encrypted on your device before being uploaded — Google cannot read your journals
  • Backups are stored in a hidden, app-only folder in your Google Drive (appdata)
  • You can sign out and switch back to local backup at any time
  • To delete Drive backup data: Google Drive settings → Manage Apps → Morning Mindful → Delete hidden app data

Data Retention

All data remains on your device until you:

  • Uninstall the app
  • Clear the app's data through your device settings
  • Delete entries within the app

Android If you use Google Drive backup, encrypted backup data remains in your Drive until you remove it via Google Drive settings. We do not retain any of your data on servers we control.

Children's Privacy

Morning Mindful is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us to have it removed.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Your Rights (GDPR — European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain data protection rights. Your data is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your journal content. You maintain full control over your data at all times. You have the right to:

  • Access: Your data is stored on your device and accessible through the app
  • Rectification: You can edit or delete your journal entries within the app
  • Erasure: You can delete all data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data
  • Data portability: Use the Export feature to download your data
  • Withdraw consent: You can stop using the app at any time and delete all data

Your Rights (CCPA — California Residents)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) provides you with specific rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Know: You have the right to know what personal information we collect. As stated above, all data is stored locally on your device.
  • Right to Delete: You can delete your data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in your device settings.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We do not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights.
  • Right to Opt-Out: We do not sell your personal information to third parties.
